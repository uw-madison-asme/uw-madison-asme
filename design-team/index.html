Design Team

About the Design Team

The ASME Design Team is back to take on an exciting new experience similar to a senior design project. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced engineer, you’ll have the chance to get certified on essential tools, gain hands-on design experience, and learn how to communicate professionally with clients.

Plus, there are leadership positions available for those passionate about making an impact this year!

What You’ll Get

  • Certification on essential engineering tools
  • Hands-on design and build experience
  • Professional client communication practice
  • Leadership opportunities
  • Real engineering project experience

Everyone is welcome to join, no matter your major or grade level.
