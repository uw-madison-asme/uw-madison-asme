Design Team

About the Design Team

The ASME Design Team takes on an exciting project similar to a senior design experience. Whether you’re a beginner or an experienced engineer, you’ll get certified on essential tools, gain hands-on design experience, and learn to communicate professionally with clients.

There are also leadership positions available for those passionate about making an impact.

What You’ll Get

  • Certification on essential engineering tools
  • Hands-on design and build experience
  • Professional client communication practice
  • Leadership opportunities

Everyone is welcome — no matter your major or grade level.
