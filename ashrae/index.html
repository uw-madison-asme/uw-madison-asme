ASHRAE — Madison Student Chapter

Welcome to ASHRAE Madison!

ASHRAE is a sub-chapter of ASME here at UW-Madison. In this club, there will be opportunities to visit an HVAC-related event such as the Charter Street Power Plant Tour, Hamel Music Center, or company building tours!

Each year companies like Trane Technologies, Findorff, 1901, and AEI come present at our general meetings to talk about their projects and internship opportunities for undergraduates.

There are also scholarships and local monthly ASHRAE meetings for members to attend to learn more about sustainability within the ASHRAE field of engineering.

  • Company tours and presentations
  • Charter Street Power Plant Tour
  • Hamel Music Center visit
  • Local ASHRAE chapter meetings
  • Scholarship opportunities
